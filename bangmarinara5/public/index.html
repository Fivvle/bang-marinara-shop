INFO

BANG MARINARA came out of a back room argument about what clothes should actually feel like. The answer: like something you chose on purpose.

We produce small runs, cut from real fabrics, sized honestly. Each piece outlasts the season that made it.

Orders ship within 3–5 business days. Returns accepted within 30 days, unworn.

ABOUT

We are a small clothing label. We work with a team of four people and two factories. We do not do seasons, we do not do drops, we do not do hype.

BANG MARINARA started because we couldn't find clothes that felt like they were made for real life. So we made them ourselves.

Every piece is designed to be worn, washed, and worn again. That is the whole idea.

Based in New York. Shipping worldwide.
